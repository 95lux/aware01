\subsection{System Requirements}
\label{sec:system-requirements}

These requirements are divided into functional requirements, which define the intended behaviour of the module, and electrical and firmware constraints, which set the boundaries dictated by the Eurorack standard and the target hardware platform.

\begin{table}[ht]
	\centering
	\caption{Functional requirements}
	\label{tab:req-functional}
	\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{@{} l X @{}}
		\toprule
		\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Requirement} \\
		\midrule
		\label{req:FR-01}FR-01 & Accept and produce stereo audio signals compatible with Eurorack signal levels \\
		\label{req:FR-02}FR-02 & Process audio in real time with sufficiently low and deterministic latency for live performance use \\
		\label{req:FR-03}FR-03 & Accept continuous control voltages and discrete gate signals conforming to the Eurorack modulation standard \\
		\label{req:FR-04}FR-04 & Provide direct physical controls for adjustment of DSP parameters and operational modes \\
		\label{req:FR-05}FR-05 & Provide visual feedback reflecting the current state of the module \\
		\label{req:FR-06}FR-06 & Audio processing and user interface tasks must not interfere with one another during concurrent operation \\
		\bottomrule
	\end{tabularx}
\end{table}
\begin{table}[ht]
	\centering
	\caption{Electrical constraints}
	\label{tab:req-electrical}
	\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{@{} l X @{}}
		\toprule
		\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Constraint} \\
		\midrule
		\label{req:EC-01}EC-01 & All signal inputs must tolerate the full Eurorack voltage range without damage to the system \\
		\label{req:EC-02}EC-02 & The analogue-to-digital conversion stage must be protected against input voltages exceeding its operating range \\
		\label{req:EC-03}EC-03 & The audio signal path must be protected against voltages and signal conditions outside the operating range of the audio front end \\
		\label{req:EC-04}EC-04 & The module must derive all operating power from the Eurorack bus and supply appropriate voltages to all subsystems \\
		\label{req:EC-05}EC-05 & The PCB layout must achieve adequate isolation between digital and analogue signal paths to meet audio performance requirements \\
		\label{req:EC-06}EC-06 & Mechanical dimensions must conform to the Eurorack 3U standard \\
		\label{req:EC-07}EC-07 & All signal inputs and outputs must use \qty{3.5}{\milli\metre} mono jack sockets conforming to the Eurorack patching standard \\
		\bottomrule
	\end{tabularx}
\end{table}
\begin{table}[ht]
	\centering
	\caption{Firmware constraints}
	\label{tab:req-firmware}
	\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{@{} l X @{}}
		\toprule
		\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Constraint} \\
		\midrule
		\label{req:FC-01}FC-01 & Audio data transfer must not be interrupted by other system activity \\
		\label{req:FC-02}FC-02 & The firmware must guarantee that time-critical audio processing is not delayed by lower-priority tasks \\
		\label{req:FC-03}FC-03 & The system must demonstrate real-time DSP capability through at least one implemented algorithm \\
		\label{req:FC-04}FC-04 & The audio codec must be configurable at runtime via the MCU \\
		\label{req:FC-05}FC-05 & User calibration data must be retained across power cycles without reliance on external storage \\
		\bottomrule
	\end{tabularx}
